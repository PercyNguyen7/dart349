Desktop Design

Players must suggest a tweet to Trevor at the beginning of every day. There are 10 days in total. The suggested tweet must be racist to cater to Trevor's racist nature.

1. Homepage displays Trevor's feed.

2. Profile page displays Trevor's profile and newest tweet.His tweets become progressively more racist.

3. Personal Data page displays Trevor's account data.

4.Latest Activities displays Trevor's latest likes/comments and the revenue we receive.

Trevor interacts with suggested posts and posts new tweets every day! Players are free to explore Trevor's feed, home, personal data and latest activities pages to further understand them.
