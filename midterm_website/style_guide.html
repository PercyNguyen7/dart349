Content

This web-based satirical game critiques the flaws of AI Algorithm in Social Media, unable to distinguish between legal and moral obligation. It equally questions the current limits of our rights to freedom of speech. With Elon Musk now as the largest shareholder of Twitter, racists rejoice and resurface on the platform.
